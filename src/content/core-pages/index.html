When your old routine stops working

Skin often changes in the same stage that sleep, mood and energy change. Drier, more reactive, duller or dry and breaking out at the same time.

This is not a failure of discipline. It is a change in the ground your routine was built on. The first step is not panic. It is recognising the pattern.
